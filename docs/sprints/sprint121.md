## Goal

The premium half of the finish fantasy: two-tone and pearl as paint-stage materials carrying
the style stat (returning what the retired paint SKUs used to carry), colour-taste coupling
on resale (a buyer persona can care about the colour), and show-finish reputation moments.

## Reuse analysis (directive 16)

**New mechanisms:** premium finish materials with style weight; a colour-taste factor on
buyer valuation; a show-finish reputation event.

**Existing mechanisms to reuse:** the Sprint 119 materials and pipeline (premium paint is a
material choice at the paint stage, not a new stage); the style stat and its Law 5 premium
path; the buyer persona taste machinery from the selling rework; the reputation event flow;
the passion-spend disclosure law for chasing show finish.

## Tasks

- T1: premium finish materials (two-tone, pearl) as paint-stage material choices, style
  weight through the existing Law 5 inputs.
- T2: colour-taste coupling in buyer valuation, persona-scoped, levers in the sign-off list.
- T3: show-finish reputation moment on selling a mint-finish car, through the existing
  reputation event flow.

## Definition of done

      passion-spend law.
- [ ] Colour taste affects only personas that carry it; levers signed before implementation.
- [ ] Coherence probes green after the style-carrier shift completes.
